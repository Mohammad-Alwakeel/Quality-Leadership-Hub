Quality Leadership Tools Hub

One file that carries four independent tools. Pick a tool on the left — each keeps its own upload flow and runs entirely in your browser, so no workbook ever leaves this page. The theme toggle in the top bar applies across every tool.

1

Assignment Distributor

Routes every active agent to a team and distributes audits by AHT-weighted workload against real analyst capacity. Weekly distribute generates a fresh book; Daily rebalance takes yesterday's export and moves only the shortfall.

2

Payments Auto Distribution

fxPick5 sampling with round-robin analyst assignment. Four views — Agent List (the final output), Notes, Tasks, and the Distribution query builder.

3

QC Audits Merger

Merges audit exports across 23 SharePoint list types with source auto-detection and date & week filtering into one clean sheet.

4

Weekly Business Report

Upload the Master Sheet once and build both Call and Non-Call reports for the previous completed week. A Classic WBR section reproduces the standard tables, plus an Enhanced Insights layer of trend, risk, momentum, Pareto and coverage charts.

Each tool is embedded in its own isolated frame, so their upload flows and internal state never collide. Switching tools keeps whatever you had loaded in the others intact for this session.
